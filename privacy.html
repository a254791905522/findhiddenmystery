Privacy Policy

Find Hidden Mystery

Effective Date: March 28, 2026
✅
Zero Data Collection Find Hidden Mystery does not collect, store, or share any personal information from its users. Your puzzle-solving experience remains entirely private.
1

About This Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to Find Hidden Mystery ("the Game", "we", "our", or "us"), a spot-the-difference puzzle game available on the Apple App Store. We created this policy to be transparent about how we handle user information — or more accurately, how we do not handle it, because we collect none. Your trust matters to us, and we designed this game with privacy at its foundation.

2

Data We Do Not Collect

Find Hidden Mystery is built with a strict no-data-collection approach. We have intentionally chosen not to gather any information from our players. This includes but is not limited to:

🚫 Personal Details No names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any form of personal identification
📡 Location Information No GPS coordinates, IP-based geolocation, or regional tracking of any kind
📈 Behavioral Analytics No gameplay patterns, session durations, tap heatmaps, or usage statistics
🖥️ Device Information No device model, operating system version, advertising identifiers, or hardware fingerprints
3

Our Privacy Commitments

  • No registration or sign-in is needed to play the game
  • No third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs are embedded
  • No advertisements that monitor or profile user behavior
  • All game progress and settings remain stored locally on your device
  • No data is transmitted to external servers or shared with third parties
  • No cookies, web beacons, or similar tracking technologies are used
4

On-Device Storage

Find Hidden Mystery stores game progress data — such as completed levels, star counts, and your settings — exclusively on your device. This data is never uploaded, synced, or transmitted anywhere. It is accessible only to you and is completely removed if you uninstall the game.

5

Third-Party Services

The game operates as a fully self-contained experience. We do not integrate any third-party services, libraries, or frameworks that collect user data. There are no social media logins, external advertising platforms, cloud analytics services, or embedded web content within the game.

6

Children's Privacy

Find Hidden Mystery carries a 4+ age rating and is designed to be enjoyed by everyone, including children. Because we collect absolutely no personal data, the game is fully compliant with children's privacy regulations including the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar international laws.

👪

We recommend that parents and guardians review the privacy practices of all applications their children use. While Find Hidden Mystery collects no data, building good digital hygiene habits starts with awareness.

7

Offline Functionality

After the initial download from the App Store, Find Hidden Mystery works entirely offline. No internet connection is required to play, which further ensures that no data can be inadvertently transmitted from your device while you enjoy the game.

8

Updates to This Policy

Should we ever need to revise this Privacy Policy — for example, due to changes in applicable laws or the addition of new features — the updated version will be published on this page with a new effective date. We encourage you to check back periodically, although our core commitment to collecting no data will remain unchanged.
